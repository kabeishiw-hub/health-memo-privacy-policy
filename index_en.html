Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 29, 2025

1. Introduction

"AI Health Memo" (hereinafter referred to as "the App") prioritizes the privacy of users' health data. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App handles.

2. Information Handled by the App

The App allows users to record the following information on their device:

  • Health data such as blood glucose levels, blood pressure, and weight (entered by users)
  • Notes and record contents
Important: All of this information is stored only on the user's device and is never transmitted to the developer's servers or third parties. The App does not collect user behavior data or usage analytics.

3. Purpose of Use

The recorded information is used for the following purposes:

  • Recording and displaying health data
  • Providing a feature to generate text for AI consultation (the generated text can be used when users ask questions to external AI services)
  • Improving user experience

4. Data Storage and Protection

All health data recorded in the App is stored locally only on the user's device. Data is never transmitted to external servers and is never shared with third parties.

Data is protected by the device's standard security features.

5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Play Services (for app distribution and updates)
  • Google Mobile Ads (for displaying advertisements)

These services have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • AdMob (Google Ads) Policy
About AI Features: The App does not transmit health data to external AI services (such as ChatGPT or Claude). The AI consultation feature is an auxiliary function that generates text for users to use when asking questions to AI services themselves.

6. User Rights

Users have the following rights:

  • The right to access their own data (viewable within the app at any time)
  • The right to delete data (all local data is deleted by uninstalling the app)
  • The right to ask questions about data usage

7. Advertising

The App uses Google Mobile Ads to display advertisements. Information such as advertising identifiers may be transmitted to the advertising network for ad delivery. For details, please see the AdMob Privacy Policy.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13 years of age and does not intentionally collect personal information from children.

9. Third-Party Data Sharing

The App will never sell, transfer, or share users' health data with third parties.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as necessary. When changes are made, we will notify users on this page and update the last updated date.
